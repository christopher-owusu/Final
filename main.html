We help you build a sustainable future

CED Practice is committed to achieving sustainable results through respectable partnerships with clients (individuals, organizations - SMEs, CSO, NGOs), to identify their highest-value opportunities, and apply appropriate strategies to address clients’ most critical development issues to bring about the needed transformations in their industries and businesses.

Our Vision

To become a market leader and respected professional services firm recognized by our clients for providing excellent development research, training, program/project management services in Africa.

Our Mission

To offer our clients with the highest quality professional services through the design and delivery of innovative and responsive development research, training and program/project management solutions for addressing their business concerns.
